1. Purpose of processing

Catchy Lab (“Company”) processes personal data to provide the SAP Development Partner service (“Service”), including:

a. Registration, identity verification, login, profile and account deletion (including grace period)
b. Development requests, interviews, proposals, FS, delivery, inquiries/reviews, offers and matching
c. AI features (external AI APIs), AI credit balance, top-up and usage records
d. Operational and marketing notifications (with consent), email and SMS
e. Support, disputes, and abuse prevention (including Experience trial reuse prevention)
f. Improvement, logs, statistics, security and incident response
g. Legal compliance


2. Categories of personal data

【Required】
· Email, password (stored hashed), name/display name
· Usage data: request IDs, submitted content and attachments, interview/proposal/AI logs, access time, IP, etc.

【Optional】
· Company name, mobile number (when verified), timezone
· Consultants: profile attachment, offer and inquiry content
· Notification consent: operational/marketing email and SMS (with consent timestamps)
· Bank transfer top-up: depositor name, amount, date as entered

【Automatic】
· Cookies/local storage: language, theme, UI preferences
· Server logs and device/browser data for security and analytics

Passwords are stored in non-reversible form. Uploads may be stored on cloud object storage (e.g. R2).
